Liu Yang

  • Associate Professor
  • Lab of Machine Learning and Data Mining
  • School of Computer Science and Technology
  • Tianjin University

Liu Yang is currently an Associate Professor with the School of Computer Science and Technology, Tianjin University. She received the Ph.D. degree from School of Computer Science and Technology, Beijing Jiaotong University, Beijing, China, in 2016. She had been a visiting scholar at Dept. of Mathematics, Hong Kong Baptist University, Hong Kong, in 2014. She had been a visiting scholar at College of Natural Sciences, Paichai University, Korea, in 2007.
